# LandBiznes Seller Guidance: How to Become a Verified Seller

Welcome to LandBiznes! This guide will walk you through the process of becoming a verified seller, the required KYC liveness checks, and how to list your land on the national registry.

## 1. How to Become a Seller
To transition from a regular user to a verified seller, follow these steps:

1.  **Register an Account**: Sign up with your email and basic details.
2.  **Complete KYC Verification**: Navigate to the "Become a Seller" or "KYC" page.
3.  **Submit Liveness Check**: Follow the active face verification instructions (see below).
4.  **Upgrade Role**: Once KYC is approved, apply for the "Owner" or "Agent" role in your profile settings.
5.  **Admin Approval**: For professional Agents, a manual review by administrators is required. Individual Owners are typically upgraded automatically upon KYC approval.

## 2. KYC Liveness & Identity Verification
Our system uses a secure, AI-powered liveness check to ensure all sellers are real individuals.

### What is Needed:
*   **Identity Document**: A clear scan or photo of your National ID, Passport, or Driver's License.
*   **Proof of Address**: A utility bill or bank statement (not older than 3 months).
*   **Active Camera**: You will need a device with a working camera for the liveness check.

### Liveness Verification Steps:
The system will capture 3 distinct profiles of your face:
1.  **Look Straight**: Align your face with the center frame.
2.  **Turn Right**: Turn your head slowly to the right.
3.  **Turn Left**: Turn your head slowly to the left.

*Behind the scenes, the system performs an automated Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and PEP screening against international watchlists to ensure regulatory compliance.*

## 3. How to List a Land Parcel
Once verified, you can list properties on the marketplace.

### Listing Requirements:
To create a valid listing, you **must** provide:
*   **Property Details**: Title, description, price (in Leones), and total size (sqm).
*   **Geospatial Data**: Accurate Latitude and Longitude coordinates.
*   **Mandatory Documents**:
    *   **Survey Plan**: A certified plan from a licensed surveyor.
    *   **Title Deed**: Proof of ownership.
    *   **Chief's/Traditional Ruler Consent**: Formal authorization from local authorities.
    *   **Land Photo**: A clean, clear photo of the actual land property.
    *   **OARG/Ministry Document (Optional)**: Official registration documents from the Office of the Administrator and Registrar General or Ministry of Lands.
    *   **Spousal Consent (Conditional)**: Required for certain property types in compliance with local regulations.

### Automatic ID Generation:
When you list a land, the system automatically assigns two unique identifiers:
1.  **ULID (Permanent Unique Land ID)**: A 26-character immutable identifier (e.g., `01KHVH...`) used for on-chain tracking and blockchain verification.
2.  **Smart Parcel ID**: A structured ID based on geographic location (e.g., `SL-8629-17403-0001`). This ID embeds the country code, a random security salt, the spatial grid ID, and the sequence number of the parcel within that grid.
